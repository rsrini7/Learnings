### MIT RLM (Recursive Language Model) – Jan 2026
- **Extends GPT-5 context window** from failing at 16-33K tokens to 1M+ tokens
- **Hybrid approach**: LLM generates Python orchestration code; deterministic execution handles loops and logic; recursive sub-instances perform text processing
- **Performance vs Cost**: On 32K-context tasks, GPT-5 alone = 0.04% accuracy ($0.16); RLM-wrapped = 58% accuracy ($0.33) = **1000x improvement**
- **Architecture insight**: Attention mechanisms alone insufficient; symbolic logic structures prevent hallucination
- **Treats long prompts as external files** (like OS virtual memory) rather than embedding directly
- References: https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.24601v1

### Correlation vs Causality – Fundamental AI Limitation – Jan 2026
- **Einstein insight (1953)**: Causality (not just pattern recognition) enabled Western science to move from observation to understanding
- **2025 Research findings**:
  - Apple's "Illusion of Thinking": Model accuracy collapses on complex reasoning tasks despite longer "thinking traces"

- **Key thesis**: "Correlation can predict; it cannot explain"
- **AI limitation**: Current LLMs are powerful pattern-matchers masquerading as cognition; lack causal models, logical reasoning, counterfactual testing, justifiable inference
- **Future direction**: Intelligence requires causal representation and logical reasoning—currently missing in LLM-based AI
