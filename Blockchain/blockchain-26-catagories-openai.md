# Comprehensive Blockchain Ecosystem

**Developer-Focused Research & Learning Map**

---

## 1. Layer 1 Blockchain Networks

### What this sector is

Base-layer blockchains providing **consensus, security, and state**.

### Subcategories

* **UTXO-based**: Bitcoin
* **Account-based (EVM)**: Ethereum, BNB Chain
* **Object / Resource-based**: Sui, Aptos (Move)
* **High-throughput runtimes**: Solana, Sei, Monad
* **Heterogeneous multi-chain**: Polkadot, Cosmos

### Technical depth to master

* Consensus (PoW, PoS, NPoS, BFT variants)
* Execution model (sequential vs parallel)
* Transaction model (UTXO vs account vs object)
* Client diversity & finality
* State growth & pruning

### Developer learning focus

* Run a full node + light client
* Write basic contracts or scripts per model
* Understand gas/fee markets
* Analyze fork-choice & finality

---

## 2. Layer 2 Scaling Solutions

### What this sector is

Systems that **inherit L1 security** while scaling execution.

### Subcategories

* **Optimistic Rollups**: Arbitrum, Optimism
* **ZK Rollups**: zkSync, StarkNet
* **Payment Channels**: Lightning, Raiden
* **Sidechains** (legacy): Polygon PoS, Gnosis

### Technical depth to master

* Fraud proofs vs validity proofs
* Sequencers & censorship risk
* Withdrawal delays & finality
* DA cost economics
* Bridge security models

### Developer learning focus

* Deploy contracts on L2
* Understand calldata posting
* Simulate fraud-proof disputes
* Compare L1 vs L2 cost profiles

---

## 3. Modular Blockchain Infrastructure

### What this sector is

Separation of **consensus, execution, DA, settlement**.

### Subcategories

* **Data Availability Layers**: Celestia, EigenDA, Avail
* **Settlement Layers**: Ethereum
* **Execution Layers**: Rollups, app-chains
* **Shared Security**: EigenLayer (restaking)

### Technical depth to master

* Data Availability Sampling (DAS)
* Erasure coding
* Shared security economics
* Slashing & correlated risk
* Modular trust assumptions

### Developer learning focus

* Study rollup stack diagrams
* Compare DA posting strategies
* Evaluate restaking risk models
