Here is a supporting content for searching or downloading research papers (with their specific uses):

### Websites and Tools 
- **Consensus**: https://get.consensus.app/neha  
  *AI-powered search and summarization tool; highlights open-access status and offers free Pro trial for research paper search.*

- **Arxiv**: https://arxiv.org/  
  *Preprint repository for free academic papers in physics, computer science, math, and more.*

- **Biorxiv**: https://www.biorxiv.org/  
  *Preprint server specializing in biology; early versions of research papers, always free.*

- **SSRN**: https://www.ssrn.com/index.cfm/en/  
  *Preprint archive for social sciences and interdisciplinary work.*

- **BASE**: https://www.base-search.net/  
  *Aggregates millions of scholarly documents from institutional repositories, many free/full-text.*


### Additional Scholarly Search and Access Methods 
- **Google Scholar**: https://scholar.google.com/  
  *Find review papers, direct download of PDFs, and citation tracking.*

- **Unpaywall**: https://unpaywall.org/  
  *Browser extension to find legal open-access versions of paywalled articles.*

- **CORE**: https://core.ac.uk/  
  *Repository and aggregator for open-access research papers/postprints.*

- **Institutional Repositories**:  
  *Examples include:*
